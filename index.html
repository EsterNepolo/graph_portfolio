About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week, hands‑on programme that demystifies practical AI tools for knowledge work. Through no‑code automation, live workshops and a capstone project, I am learning to:

  • Automate document, spreadsheet & data workflows
  • Organise knowledge and conduct literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots for project support
  • Create professional visuals & presentations
  • Apply generative AI ethically and strategically within teams
